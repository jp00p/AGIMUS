**QUIZ INFO**
You get bonus points for being slightly wrong!
You get bonus points for being the first to get it right!
You get extra bonus points for being first + silly!
Correctitude is calculated using: <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levenshtein_distance>
Only one quiz can be active at a time

**QUIZ COMMANDS**
`/quiz start <show (optional)>` - start a round of Quiz!
`/quiz answer <title>` - provide an answer for the current Quiz round
`/quiz report` - see the fuzziness of the last quiz answers (you want both numbers between 72-80 for the bonus)
`/scores` - see the top scores
`/profile` - see your profile card
`/info <show> <episode (s##e##)>` - Get information about a specific episode!

**TRIVIA COMMANDS**
`/trivia` - get a random trivia question!
`/triva <category>` - get trivia from a specific category (categories listed in dropdown)
