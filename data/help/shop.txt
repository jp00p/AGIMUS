**SHOP INFO**
A silly shop to waste your points on!
All proceeds go directly to the jackpot.

**COMMANDS**
`!shop [profiles|badges|roles]` - list all profile cards, badges or roles for sale
`!buy [profile|badge|role] [##]` - buy a profile card, badge or role
`/profile` - see your profile card
`!info [tng|voy|ds9|friends|firefly|simpsons|enterprise|tos|lowerdecks|disco|picard|tas|sunny] [s##e##]` - Get information about a specific episode!
